About Catering


Tradition since 1957

Dindigul Thalappakatti, renowned for its authentic biryani, has been a staple in Tamil Nadu since 1957. Founded by Nagasamy Naidu as Anandha Vilas Biriyani Hotel in Dindigul, the restaurant quickly became famous for its unique style of biryani, prepared with aromatic spices and a signature blend of tender meat. The name "Thalappakatti," meaning "turban" in Tamil, symbolizes the restaurant’s deep-rooted tradition and heritage. Over the years, Dindigul Thalappakatti has expanded globally with over 100 outlets, including 92 in India and 9 internationally. A trusted name for biryani lovers, the restaurant continues to serve generations of customers with its delicious and flavorful dishes.
